How to play

Try to survive in a toxic environment!.

  • First, create or join a party.
  • Use the left and right arrow keys to change the direction of the cell.
  • Drop poison with the space key.

Rules

  • Touching poison or virus will kill you.
  • Increase your score by moving in the board.
  • If you succeed to kill a virus, get 50% of his score.
